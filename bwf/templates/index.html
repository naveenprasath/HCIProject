Why Bills with friends?

Bills with friends is a simple tool to track debts and manage shared bills amongst friends. Signing up is free!


The story behind this project

We group of 4 Graduate Students. It was Human Computer Interaction course. The professor asked us to

implement an application with our own idea. We came up with an idea of building a mobile/ Web

application to organize the bills with our friends. Consider there are 4 friend stays together. They

go to movies, restaurants buys groceries and even takes trips together. They want to track how much

each spends, so that the other guys owes him back. There are few apps in market.But they are not very

good. I want to make a improved version with many visualizations. With this app, we can store small

description of the bill (which includes event name, location, date and time), split the bill among 4

and gives a graphical representation of records in the form of chart, graph, etc.
